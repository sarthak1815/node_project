Our Story

Founded in 2010, LuxeDrive began with a simple vision: to make luxury car rentals accessible to everyone in India. What started as a small fleet of 10 vehicles in Mumbai has grown into a nationwide network with over 500 premium cars across 50+ locations.

We've served over 10,000 satisfied customers, from business executives to families on vacation, wedding parties to corporate events. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and exceptional service has made us India's most trusted luxury car rental brand.

Today, we continue to innovate, adding electric vehicles to our fleet and leveraging technology to provide seamless booking experiences.
